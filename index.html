Culture Recovery Fund Grants

The UK government is providing Â£500m of grants to "cultural organisations" in order to help them recover from the impact of COVID-19. This page attempts to analyse the data on these grants provided so far.

Organisation names provided in the data release by Arts Council England have been semi-automatically matched to Companies House and Charity Commission data, as well as other sources, in order to classify them. Beneficial ownership data is used where possible, and limited companies more than 75% owned by non-profit entities are considered non-profits.

The data used can be viewed here — please suggest a change if you spot an error.
